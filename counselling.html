TS EAMCET Counselling Process







Step 1: Registration & Fee Payment

After results, visit the official counselling site (https://tseamcet.nic.in). Pay the processing fee (₹1200 for OC/BC, ₹600 for SC/ST) using online payment methods. Then book your slot for document verification.

Step 2: Certificate Verification

Visit the allotted Help Line Center (HLC) with original documents and 2 photocopy sets. You’ll be issued a verification slip with login credentials.

  • TS EAMCET Hall Ticket & Rank Card
  • SSC & Intermediate Marks Memos
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Caste/Income/Residence Certificates (if applicable)

Step 3: Choice Filling (Web Options)

Login using your credentials and fill in choices of colleges and branches in order of preference. You can edit them until the locking date. Priority in selection depends on your rank, category, and seat availability.

Example Web Option Entries:

  • JNTU Hyderabad - CSE
  • OU College of Engineering - ECE
  • CBIT - IT
  • Vasavi College - CSE (AI)
  • MGIT - Mech
  • CVR College - EEE

Tip: Choose at least 30–50 options to increase your chances.

Step 4: Seat Allotment

The system allots seats based on your rank, preferences, and reservation status. You can check your result online and download your seat allotment order.

Step 5: Fee Payment & Reporting

Pay the tuition fee online if not eligible for fee reimbursement. Download the joining report and allotment letter. Report to the allotted college physically before the deadline with your documents.

Step 6: Final Phase / Spot Round

If you missed earlier rounds or want to upgrade, participate in the second or spot round counselling. Spot admissions help fill vacant seats (mostly in private colleges).

Note: Spot round admissions are often direct, and fee reimbursement may not apply.
