B.Tech Courses

B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology) focuses on practical applications of engineering concepts, and is more skill-development and lab-oriented.

  • Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
  • Information Technology (IT)
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Artificial Intelligence & Data Science
  • Cyber Security
  • Robotics and Automation
  • Biotechnology
  • Agricultural Engineering
  • Food Technology
  • Environmental Engineering

Future Roadmaps

Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)

Subjects: Data Structures and Algorithms, Operating Systems, Database Management Systems, Computer Networks, Software Engineering, Programming Languages (Java, Python, C++), Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning.

Future Roadmap: Software Development, Data Science, AI Engineering, Cybersecurity, Full Stack Development, Cloud Computing, Research, Entrepreneurship, Higher Studies (MS, PhD).

Civil Engineering

Subjects: Structural Analysis, Construction Materials, Fluid Mechanics, Geotechnical Engineering, Transportation Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Surveying.

Future Roadmap: Construction Management, Structural Engineering, Transportation Planning, Geotechnical Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Urban Planning, Infrastructure Development, Government Jobs, Consulting.

Mechanical Engineering

Subjects: Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Manufacturing Processes, Machine Design, Control Systems, Robotics, Heat Transfer.

Future Roadmap: Automotive Industry, Aerospace, Manufacturing, Robotics, Energy Sector, Research and Development, Design Engineering, Consulting.

Agriculture Courses

Agriculture courses focus on farming, food production, crop science, animal husbandry, and rural development. Great for government jobs, agri-business, and environmental sectors.

  • B.Sc Agriculture
  • B.Sc Horticulture
  • B.Sc Forestry
  • B.Tech Agricultural Engineering
  • B.Tech Food Technology
  • B.Sc Animal Husbandry
  • B.F.Sc (Fisheries Science)
  • B.Sc Sericulture
  • B.Sc Agricultural Marketing and Co-operation

Future Roadmaps

B.Sc Agriculture

Subjects: Agronomy, Soil Science, Plant Breeding, Agricultural Economics, Entomology, Plant Pathology, Extension Education.

Future Roadmap: Agricultural Officer, Farm Manager, Agronomist, Soil Scientist, Agricultural Consultant, Research Scientist, Government Jobs, Agri-business, Higher Studies (M.Sc. Agriculture).

B.Tech Agricultural Engineering

Subjects: Farm Machinery, Irrigation Engineering, Soil and Water Conservation, Post-Harvest Technology, Renewable Energy in Agriculture.

Future Roadmap: Agricultural Engineer, Farm Machinery Designer, Irrigation Specialist, Research and Development, Manufacturing, Government Jobs, Higher Studies (M.Tech).

B.E Courses

B.E (Bachelor of Engineering) is slightly more theory-based than B.Tech, but covers the same core concepts. It is offered by traditional universities and focuses on strong engineering fundamentals.

  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechatronics
  • Automobile Engineering
  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Aerospace Engineering
  • Marine Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Mining Engineering

Future Roadmaps

B.E. Computer Science Engineering

Subjects: Programming, Data Structures, Algorithms, Operating Systems, Database Systems, Networks, Software Engineering.

Future Roadmap: Software Development, System Architect, Database Administrator, Network Engineer, AI/ML Engineer, Cybersecurity Expert, Consultant, Research.

B.E. Mechanical Engineering

Subjects: Thermodynamics, Solid Mechanics, Fluid Mechanics, Manufacturing, Machine Design, Control Systems.

Future Roadmap: Design Engineer, Manufacturing Engineer, Automotive Engineer, Aerospace Engineer, Energy Engineer, Robotics Engineer, Consultant.
